# LectureGenius - Video Demonstration Script

## Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:10)
**Visual:** Start on the **Dashboard** (Home Page).
**Action:** Cursor glides over the "Record Lecture" and "My Recordings" cards.
**Voiceover:** "Welcome to LectureGenius. Ideally designed for students and professionals, this AI-powered tool captures, summarizes, and translates your lectures in real-time."

## Scene 2: Settings & Setup (0:10 - 0:20)
**Visual:** Click on **Settings** in the sidebar.
**Action:** 
1. Show the **"Translation"** dropdown and select *Spanish*.
2. Scroll down to show the **"Course Management (LMS)"** section with the "Coming Soon" badge.
**Voiceover:** "Customize your experience. Set your preferred translation language and prepare for our upcoming LMS integration that syncs directly with your university portal."

## Scene 3: Recording a Lecture (0:20 - 0:45)
**Visual:** Click on **Record Lecture**.
**Action:** 
1. Enter a title: *"Introduction to Quantum Mechanics"*.
2. Select **Spoken Language** as *"English"*.
3. Click the **Red Record Button**.
4. *Simulate speaking:* "Quantum mechanics is a fundamental theory in physics that provides a description of the physical properties of nature at the scale of atoms and subatomic particles." (Watch live captions appear).
5. Click **Stop**.
**Voiceover:** "Capturing knowledge is effortless. Just hit record. LectureGenius listens to the audio, providing live captions and processing the content securely."

## Scene 4: Analysis & Translation (0:45 - 1:10)
**Visual:** The "Lecture Processed!" screen appears.
**Action:**
1. Scroll down to show the **English Summary** and **Key Points**. (Highlight that comprehension is always in English).
2. Scroll to the **Translation Section**.
3. Ensure *Spanish* is selected and click **Translate**.
4. Show the Spanish notes appearing.
**Voiceover:** "Instantly receive a structured English summary and key points to ensure core comprehension. Need it in your native language? Generate a high-quality translation with a single click."

## Scene 5: Auto-Sync Schedule (1:10 - 1:20)
**Visual:** Click on **Sync Schedule** in the sidebar.
**Action:** Show the "Coming Soon" page. Click the dropdown to show "Canvas", "Blackboard", etc.
**Voiceover:** "And coming soon: Auto-Sync. Connect your Canvas or Blackboard account, and LectureGenius will automatically join and record your Zoom or Teams classes for you."

## Scene 6: Conclusion (1:20 - 1:30)
**Visual:** Click on **About**.
**Action:** Show the "LectureGenius" logo and "Developed by Ramkumar" section.
**Voiceover:** "LectureGenius. Smarter notes for a smarter future. Developed by Ramkumar."
